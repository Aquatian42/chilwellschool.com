Welcome Back!

Please see below some important updates/ reminders:

𝗣𝘂𝗽𝗶𝗹 𝗣𝗿𝗲𝗺𝗶𝘂𝗺 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗙𝗿𝗲𝗲 𝗦𝗰𝗵𝗼𝗼𝗹 𝗠𝗲𝗮𝗹𝘀: If your child is eligible for ‘free school meals’ and you register them for this, we’ll receive extra funding called ‘pupil premium’. We use this extra money to improve the educational provision and resources at the school.

At Chilwell School, we get £1,050 funding for every eligible pupil who is registered for free school meals. This extra money could make a real difference to the quality of education we offer.

We want to make sure as many eligible pupils as possible are claiming their free school meals. We use the Department for Education's free school meals eligibility checking system to determine eligibility.

To apply, you will need to register and provide:
Your National Insurance Number or National Asylum Support Service Reference Number
Your last name
Your date of birth

If your entitlement to free school meals cannot be assessed through the eligibility checking system, they will let you know.

You can apply for free school meals online (link sent via Arbor) or by contacting us on 0300 500 80 80.

𝗣𝗮𝗿𝗸𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗥𝗲𝗺𝗶𝗻𝗱𝗲𝗿: Please ensure that when dropping off or collecting your children, you do not park on the one-way system, on the red double lines, or in front of fire doors. Recently, we have noticed an increase in inappropriate parking, which has caused health and safety concerns for our school community. Your cooperation is essential in keeping everyone safe.

Appointments: Meetings with teachers or staff must be arranged in advance. Parents and carers will not be seen without a scheduled appointment.

Signing In: All visitors must report to reception and sign in upon arrival.

Accessing the School: No one should attempt to enter the school premises without authorisation, even after the school day has finished.

Thank you for helping us uphold the highest standards of safety and security for our school community.
