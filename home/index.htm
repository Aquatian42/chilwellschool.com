Welcome from Our Head Teacher

Welcome to Chilwell School, where we are dedicated to providing the best opportunities to foster success in our students. We want everyone involved with Chilwell School to enjoy the pleasure of learning and the excitement of new challenges.

Our curriculum is broad and challenging, focusing on developing key skills. We know that a strong education is based upon the building blocks of core skills, high challenge, and a breadth of opportunity. A Chilwell education ensures that our students make strong progress to be confident adults.

Andy Palmer

The Governing Body was delighted when Ofsted recognised Chilwell School as a happy and friendly school. This is a testament to the dedication and hard work of our staff, who strive to create an environment where every student can thrive and achieve their full potential. Chilwell School is at the heart of our community, providing a space for young people to grow, learn, and develop into confident individuals. Through the combined efforts of our staff, students, parents, and wider community, we nurture young minds that will go on to make a positive impact both locally and beyond.

Lucy

Hi, I’m Lucy! I started here in 2021 and I’m now in year 10. The school has loads of supportive teachers and staff and some great facilities. I’m really enjoying doing German and Science for my GCSEs and I’m hoping to go to the 6th form here.

Charlotte
