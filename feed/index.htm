This week, Chavwell School hosted an incredible Careers Fair in our Lakeview building, and the energy was truly inspiring! The event brought together representatives from a wide range of organisations, including the Royal Air Force, Experian, the Navy, Nottingham Trent University (NTU), and Nottingham College, among many others.

The Careers Fair provided our students with the chance to explore a variety of potential career paths, ask questions, and gather insights into the opportunities available to them after school. Whether their interests lie in higher education, vocational training, or direct career entry, there was something for everyone.

It was amazing to see our students engaging so enthusiastically with the representatives, sparking conversations about their aspirations and goals. Events like these play a vital role in broadening horizons and encouraging young people to think about their futures with excitement and confidence.

We’d like to extend a massive thank you to all the organisations and individuals who participated and shared their time, knowledge, and expertise with our students.

Special thanks also go to the staff and volunteers whose hard work ensured the event ran smoothly.

At Chavwell School, we are committed to empowering our students with the tools and knowledge they need to make informed decisions about their futures. The Careers Fair was a wonderful example of this commitment in action, and we’re so proud of the opportunities it provided for our students.

Questions and answers

Please could you re-send guidance on how parents can see Google classrooms? It’s tricky with more than one child to get an overview.

Teachers can amend the settings for the course so that parents can see “guardian summaries”. Parents will receive emails when this is switched on by the subject teacher.

Is there an ipay app?

Yes, iPay has recently developed a parent app, making school payments easier for parents. Download the app ‘iPayimpact’ from the App Store or Google Play.

I wish Ipayimpact would do an auto top up when the account is low?

With iPayimpact, you can set up automated payments daily, weekly, or monthly to ensure your child’s account always has adequate funds.

How do we get the Unifrog login for pupils please?

Students have been issued unifrog logins. For students who need a reminder, they should contact student services.

How do you report absence if you have a hospital appointment? Arbor only allows on the day reporting?

Please send any future appointment information to  absence@chilwellschool.co.uk – see here for further information.
